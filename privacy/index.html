Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

MarketingReports.io ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at marketingreports.io. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site.

Information We Collect

We collect information automatically through standard web analytics tools, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Referring URLs and exit pages
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • IP address (anonymized where possible)

We do not collect names, email addresses, or payment information directly. If you contact us via email, we retain that correspondence to respond to your inquiry.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager) to understand how visitors use the site. Google may set cookies to track sessions and behavior across sessions. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.
  • Affiliate tracking: When you click an affiliate link on our site, the destination company (e.g. Whatagraph, Databox, Supermetrics) may set cookies to track referrals for commission attribution. We do not control these cookies.
  • Advertising (future): We may run paid advertising campaigns on platforms including Google Ads, Meta Ads, and LinkedIn Ads. These platforms may use pixels or tags to measure ad performance and enable remarketing to prior visitors. You can manage your ad preferences through your Google account, Facebook settings, or LinkedIn settings.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that some site functionality may be affected.

Affiliate Links

MarketingReports.io participates in affiliate programs. When you click a link to a third-party tool or service and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial coverage — we only recommend tools we have evaluated. See our Affiliate Disclosure for full details.

Third-Party Services

Our site may link to or embed content from third-party services. These services operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review:

  • Google Analytics / Google Tag Manager
  • Netlify (hosting infrastructure)
  • Affiliate program platforms (Impact, ShareASale, and partner-specific programs)

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained according to the default settings of each analytics platform (typically 26 months for Google Analytics). We do not sell or rent any collected data to third parties.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other applicable laws to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you. To make a request, email us at hello@marketingreports.io.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our site evolves. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
