What Makes a Good LinkedIn Ads Dashboard?

LinkedIn Ads templates should cover CPL (cost per lead), CTR, CPM, engagement rate, conversion rate by campaign type (Sponsored Content vs InMail vs Dynamic Ads), audience demographics (job title, industry, company size), and budget pacing. Unlike Google Ads, LinkedIn doesn't have a native Looker Studio connector — you'll need a third-party connector for all templates below. Most offer 14-day free trials.

Do I Need a Paid Connector for LinkedIn Ads?

Yes — LinkedIn Ads does not have a native Looker Studio connector. All templates below require a third-party connector like Supermetrics, Porter Metrics, Catchr, or Coupler.io. Most charge $15–50/month per destination after a free trial.

The good news: Porter Metrics offers a free forever plan (up to 3 accounts with 30 days of history). For agencies managing multiple LinkedIn accounts, paid plans are worth the cost to avoid manual CSV exports.
