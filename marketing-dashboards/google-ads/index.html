What Makes a Good Google Ads Dashboard Template?

The best templates track the metrics that actually drive decisions: impressions, clicks, CTR, CPC, conversions, cost per conversion, and ROAS. They should connect directly to your Google Ads account (not require manual CSV exports), update automatically, and be filterable by campaign, ad group, keyword, and device. Some templates below are fully free — others require a paid connector like Supermetrics, Catchr, or Coupler.io. We note cost clearly on each card.

Free vs Paid: Which Template Should You Use?

Most free templates use Google's native connector — which means they only work with Google Ads data and don't require a paid subscription. If your reporting needs are straightforward (one account, standard KPIs), these are usually enough.

Templates marked Free* require a third-party connector like Supermetrics or Catchr — free to trial, but paid to keep. These are worth it if you also need Facebook Ads, LinkedIn, or other non-Google data in the same report.
