Free vs Paid Connectors for Organic Social

Instagram, Facebook Pages, and LinkedIn Pages all block native Looker Studio connections — you need a third-party connector. Data Bloo offers some genuinely free connectors (Instagram Insights, LinkedIn Pages). Porter Metrics, Coupler.io, and Supermetrics have free trials then charge $15–50/month.

If you only need one platform, start with a free connector from Data Bloo. For multi-platform reporting or agency use, a paid connector that covers all channels is worth the monthly cost.
