What Makes a Good Search Console Dashboard Template?

A good GSC template goes beyond raw impressions and clicks. It should show CTR by page and query, average position trends, brand vs non-brand query splits, short-tail vs long-tail performance, page ranking distribution (page 1 vs page 2), and filter by device and country. Most templates below use Google's free native connector — though some paid connectors unlock combined query + page analysis that the native connector can't do.

Free Native vs Paid Connector: What's the Difference?

Google's free native Search Console connector is built into Looker Studio and works immediately — but it splits data into two separate sources (site queries and URL queries) which can't be combined. This means you can't filter by query on the pages view, or vice versa.

Paid connectors like Supermetrics, Windsor.ai, and Catchr unify these into one data source, enabling richer cross-filtering. If you do regular SEO reporting, a paid connector is usually worth it. Most offer 14-day free trials.
