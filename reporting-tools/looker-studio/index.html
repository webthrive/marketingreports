What Is Looker Studio?

Looker Studio is Google's free business intelligence and data visualization platform. Originally launched as Google Data Studio in 2016 and rebranded in 2022, it lets marketers build interactive dashboards and reports by connecting to Google Ads, GA4, Search Console, BigQuery, and hundreds of other sources.

It's entirely free — no paid tiers, no seat limits. For teams already deep in the Google ecosystem, it's often the best starting point for marketing reporting.

Key Features

  • Completely free — unlimited reports, unlimited sharing, no credit card
  • Native Google integrations — GA4, Google Ads, Search Console, YouTube, BigQuery
  • Community connectors — 600+ third-party connectors via the connector gallery
  • Blended data sources — combine multiple sources in a single chart
  • Live shareable links — share interactive dashboards without exports
  • Looker Studio Pro — paid upgrade ($9/user/month) adds team workspaces and SLA support
  • Template gallery — hundreds of free community templates

Where Looker Studio Falls Short

  • No automated PDF report scheduling on the free plan
  • Non-Google connectors often require paid third-party tools (e.g. Supermetrics)
  • Can be slow with large datasets
  • Limited customer support — community forums only on free tier
  • No white-labeling on free plan
