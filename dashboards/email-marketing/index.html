What Makes a Good Email Marketing Dashboard?

Email marketing templates should track open rate, click rate, click-to-open rate (CTOR), unsubscribe rate, bounce rate, deliverability, list growth, and revenue per email. The most useful templates blend email performance with ecommerce data (Shopify, WooCommerce) to show actual revenue attribution — not just open rates.

Does My ESP Have a Native Looker Studio Connector?

No — Mailchimp, Klaviyo, HubSpot, and ActiveCampaign do not have native Looker Studio connectors. All templates below require a third-party connector like Supermetrics, Porter, Catchr, or Coupler.io.

Klaviyo and Mailchimp connectors from Porter Metrics start at $15/month. For agencies managing multiple email accounts, Supermetrics' multi-account plans are more cost-effective.
